# Adding a New Custom Page

![rs-crud-addnewcustompage.webp](/assets/manage-page-fields/rs-crud-addnewcustompage.webp)

The **Add New Page Action** dialog allows you to manually expand your application beyond the automatically scaffolded pages.

- **Page Name**: Enter a unique identifier for the new page.
    
- **Page Type**: Choose the functional template for the page, such as a **List**, **Add**, or **View** type.
    
- **Menu Integration**:
    
    - **Add To Sub Menu**: Automatically nests the new page under its parent table in the application menu.
        
    - **Add To Main Menu**: Places the page at the top level of your application's navigation sidebar.
